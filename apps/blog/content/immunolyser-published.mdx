Getting a paper published feels different than I expected. There's no fanfare — just a quiet email saying your manuscript has been accepted.

## What is Immunolyser?

Immunolyser is an automated web-based pipeline for immunopeptidomics data analysis. It standardises what was previously a fragmented, manual process — bringing together peptide length distribution, motif analysis, sequence clustering (via GibbsCluster), and MHC binding affinity prediction (via NetMHCpan) into a single reproducible workflow.

## Why It Matters

Every lab had their own scripts. Results weren't reproducible. New researchers faced a steep learning curve. Immunolyser wraps the entire workflow into a web interface. Upload your data, select your parameters, get publication-ready figures.

## The Publication Journey

The paper was submitted to **Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal (CSBJ)**. The review process took about 3 months:

1. **Initial submission** — June 2025
2. **First review** — Major revisions requested
3. **Revision** — Added murine dataset validation
4. **Acceptance** — October 2025

The reviewers pushed us to make the paper significantly better. Their feedback on validating across species strengthened our claims considerably.
