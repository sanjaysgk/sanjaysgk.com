Hey, I'm **Sanjay** — a bioinformatician and research assistant at **Monash University's Biomedicine Discovery Institute**, where I work on cancer neoantigen discovery and immunopeptidomics.

I built **CAN-IMMUNE**, a comprehensive platform documenting 1.2M+ mutant peptides across 33 cancer types, and co-authored **Immunolyser**, a published web-based immunopeptidomics analysis pipeline.

### Journey

I started as a civil engineering student in Bangalore. In 2016, I discovered programming and never looked back. I self-taught my way from AutoCAD to Python through 50+ online courses, then moved to Melbourne for a **Master of Data Science at Monash University**.

Along the way, I worked as a Data Scientist at Audacix and Data Analyst at Eastern Health, applying ML to real-world healthcare problems.

### What I'm Building

By night, I ship side projects:

- **Scrollpaper** — A mobile app that distills scientific papers into bite-sized daily reads. Swipe through science, not social media.
- **LabScrib** — An iOS voice recording and AI-powered transcription app for academics and researchers.
- A **bioinformatics SaaS** platform making computational pipelines less painful.

### This Blog

I write about bioinformatics, computational biology, HPC workflows, building products, and the intersection of science and code. The goal is to document what I learn and build in public.

This blog is built with **Next.js** and powered by [Fumadocs](https://fumadocs.vercel.app/). It's part of a [monorepo](https://github.com/sanjaysgk/sanjaysgk.com) alongside my portfolio site.
